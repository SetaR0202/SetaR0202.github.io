Seta Rakotomandimby

seta.rakotomandimby@enpc.fr

I am a Phd student under the supervision of Michel DE LARA at Cermics lab at École nationale des ponts et chaussées in Champs-sur-Marne, France.

I graduated from the highly recognized engineering school ENSTA Paris in applied mathematics and from the Parisian operational research master MPRO.

My PhD subject is Algorithms in generalized convexity with application to sparse optimization.

My research topics are losses based on subdifferential representations for multiclassification in machine learning and nonconvex dual methods to solve sparse minimization problems.
